# Significant Enhancements in Core Capabilities: More Stable, Smoother, More Realistic! 

Beyond just multi-modal input, Seedance 2.0 has been significantly enhanced on a fundamental level. Physical laws are more reasonable, action performances are more natural and smooth, instruction understanding is more precise, and style consistency is more stable. Not only can it steadily complete high-difficulty generation tasks like complex actions and continuous motion, but it also makes the overall video effect more realistic and smooth. This is a comprehensive evolution of underlying capabilities!
