# Creative Templates / Precise Replication of Complex Effects
Not only can it generate images and write stories, but Seedance 2.0 also supports "mimicking"—creative transitions, commercial cuts, movie clips, and complex editing. As long as you have reference images or videos, the model can recognize action rhythms, camera language, and visual structures, replicating them accurately. It doesn't matter if you don't know professional terminology; just clearly write what part you want to reference, such as "Reference the rhythm and camera movement of @Video1, and the character styling of @Image1", and the model can generate a high-quality version of yours. Be bold and try! It really can do it.
