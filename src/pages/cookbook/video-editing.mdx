## High Usability of Video Editing
Sometimes you already have a video and don't want to find images or redo it from scratch; you just hope to adjust a specific short segment of action, extend it by a few seconds, or make the character's performance closer to your ideas. Now you can directly use the existing video as input to make targeted modifications to specified segments, actions, or rhythms without changing the rest of the content. You can quickly complete adjustments without generating it from scratch.
