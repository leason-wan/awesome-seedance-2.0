# High Difficulty/Controllable Camera Movement and Precise Action Replication 
Previously, if you wanted the model to imitate the positioning, camera movement, or complex actions in a movie, you either had to write a bunch of detailed prompt words or simply couldn't do it. But now, all you need is to upload a reference video, and you are good to go.

<CookbookCase 
  client:load
  title="Case 1"
  prompt={`Referencing the man's image from @Image1, he is in the elevator from @Image2. Completely reference all the camera movements and the protagonist's facial expressions from @Video1. The protagonist has a Hitchcock zoom when frightened, followed by a few wrap-around shots showing the perspective inside the elevator. The elevator doors open, follow the camera out of the elevator. The scene outside the elevator refers to @Image3. The man looks around, referencing @Video1 using a robotic arm to follow the character's line of sight from multiple angles.`}
  inputAssets={[
    { type: 'image', url: 'https://cdn.weniai.com/seedance/3-1-1.png' },
